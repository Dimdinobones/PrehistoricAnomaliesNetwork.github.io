Isla Lozano

Isla Lozano is the first island bought by Wesley, and is the permanent home to the majority of the relocated prehostoric animals; it is also the main site of our opperations. Isla Lozano is a tropical island off the coast of The Bahamas, with lush forests and wetlands, it currently contains non-avian dinosaurs, pterosaurs, and marine repriles. The isle has since been evacuated of all the locals to make way for our opperations as we aim to minimise human impact on this ecosystem in hopes to prevent the transmissions of disease and the illegal smuggling of PAN property. We have set four bases on Isla Lozano, each housing a medbay to attend to injured animals, and a communications tower to broadcast signals, photos and videos of the island over to our base on the mainland. There is little to no humans on Isla Lozano, the only people allowed on the island are Wesley and PAN employees in the event of an emergency.
