Isla Oasis

Isla Oasis is an uninhabited coastal desert island east of Egypt and is currently in the progress of being set up as our second island for our opperation. It is filled with dunes and dryland forests, along with numerus floodplains that are regulalry filled with rainfall that sustain plentiful vegetation. Isla Oasis is intended to house our permian animals which include synapids and dyapsids such as Diictiodon, Postosuchus, Gorgonops and Scutosaurus. This island will also include triassic dinosaurs such as Plateosaurus, Ceolophysis, and Herrerasaurus; and other animals more adapted to desert environments such as Tarbosaurus, Velociraptor, and Gallimimus. We are currently constructing our bases for Isla Oasis in which the island should be open for relocation by late 2046.
