Isla Polar

Isla Polar is a tundra island north of Russia and is our planned third island for our relocation program. We have partnered with the russian government and have established ownership of the island in response to the world wide ban on prehistoric animals. Isla Polar contains warmer taiga forests with connifers and pine trees, as well as mountain ranges and vast lakes that contain numerous species of salmon and trout. Isla Polar is planned to house the cenozoic prehistoirc animals, which include mammals such as Mammoths, Smilodons, Dire wolves, and Glyptodons; and additional cenozoic animals including Terror birds and Dodos. We also intend on housing arctic dwelling animals such as Cryolophosaurus, Pachyrhinosaurus, and Cryodrakon. We will begin construction by 2047 and our planned due date is early 2051.
